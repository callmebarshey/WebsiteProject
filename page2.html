THREADING:

The threading services that we provide are not limited to just eyebrows. We perform all kind of facial threading services. Full face threading, upper lip, cheeks, chin, forehead etc are some of the examples. Threading is an old Indian method of hair removal that does not involve any chemical but rather single piece of cotton thread tied together to remove the hair. After cleaning the required area, applying some powder to decrease friction the thread is twisted and pulled along the areas were unwanted hair has grown. It removes the hair from the root instead of creating splits between hair follicles.

WAXING:

The waxing services that we provide are not limited to body waxing. We perform all kind of facial waxing services as well. Waxing is a good technique for hair removal for clients who are not able to hair the pain of threading and when large area of hair growth like legs or arms have to be removed. The process of waxing uses a strip wax that is applied to your skin and removed with strip. We use chocolate wax and creamed based wax for facial services as they are more gentle towards the sensitive areas of the face. For body wax- we use honey wax and hard wax. Unlike regular strip waxes that require strip for hair removal, Hard wax hardens up quite fast and could be removed without using any strip. The only requirement for waxing is that the hair growth should be minimum of 0.2 inches long for the esthetician to be able to grab the hair.
