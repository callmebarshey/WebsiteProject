Why threading?

Hey lovelies, glad you are here. We are glad you are taking Threading as an option of hair removal. If you’ve ever had your brows shaped before, you know threading is the most popular and beneficial one. One can almost never be allergic from Thread.

Unlike Waxes, thread does not produce any zest or bumps around your skin. And let’s be honest epilators are way too painful for you. Threading has gained vast popularity in recent years, but honestly speaking so much about the hair-removal practice is still a mystery to many. When we established this salon in 2004, we had so many clients come to us everyday asking about what on the earth is threading. . But as the pioneers in this brow artistry, we take pride in providing you expert advice on the dos and don’ts for threading. Everything you need to know in this one stop.

The threading technique gives the Brow Artist complete control and precision of the hair-removal process. Wax is messy and usually the brow-artist does not have a control over what portion of the wax paper would cover the brows. We have seen great brow artist miss a spot or over waxed an eyebrow. But while threading the esthetician can remove a single hair or lines of hair thus providing you with any arch and any shape you desire.

Brow and glow the pioneer in the threading category, having opened their first location in 2004, we have then promised to give the best customer service and offer the best pricing for threading, tinting, waxing and henna services.
